About

My research focuses on the design, fabrication, and validation of acoustic and microfluidic platforms that can manipulate biological samples across multiple scales, from nanoparticles and extracellular vesicles to cells and tissue-level delivery interfaces.

I am particularly interested in translating acoustofluidic systems into practical biomedical tools for cytopathological screening, cancer liquid biopsy, single-cell analysis, and minimally invasive therapy.
